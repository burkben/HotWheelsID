Redline ID

Support

Redline ID is a free, open-source companion for the discontinued Hot Wheels id Race Portal. It supports iPhone and iPad and does not require an account.

Quick checks

  • Turn on the Race Portal before tapping Connect portal.
  • Allow Bluetooth access when iOS asks.
  • Keep the iPhone or iPad close to the portal during the first connection.
  • Use Demo mode on the Speed tab to explore the app without portal hardware.
  • If a connection stops responding, power-cycle the portal and reconnect.
Need help or found a bug?

Open a public issue with your device model, iOS version, portal firmware if known, and the steps that led to the problem.

Open a support issue

Compatibility

Live telemetry requires a physical Hot Wheels id Race Portal and Bluetooth. Redline ID supports both known legacy and modern portal firmware transports. Demo mode, the garage, history, and app settings can be reviewed without hardware.
